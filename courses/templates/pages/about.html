OYGo is a web grading system built by Sam Gralla. Students grade their own work according to a rubric, and a grader reviews it. This helps students take an active role in assessing weaknesses, which builds strength. OYGo: own your growth!

This is the very first version of OYGo. It's not too snazzy but it works. (And if it doesn't, email me!)
